Updated version of Gesture Control
## The following features are now available and tested almost successfully in Combination.py
#Lefthand
* Volume up-down: use your index and thumb to increase the volume, your middle and thumb to decrease the volume.
* Zoom in-out: use your ring and thumb to zoom in, your pinky and thumb to zoom out.

#Righthand
* Scroll up-down: raise your index finger to scroll up, lower your index finger to scroll down.
* Switch tab: raise both your index and pinky finger to switch tabs.
* Page refresh: use your thumb and ring finger to refresh webpage.
* Turn off webcam: use your thumb and pinky finger to turn off your webcam. 
* Moving and clicking, dragging.

Gaming mode
-----------
- For now you can use arrow keys to control a game, fully open your hand and point in direction which correspond to arrow key you want to press
